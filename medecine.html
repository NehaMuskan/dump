Donate your Medicine

Please consider donating following medicine, ( please do check expiry date before donating medicines and also avoid donation of near to expiry medicines)

  • Fever
  • Blood Pressure
  • Abdominal Pain
  • Diarrhoea
  • Ear ache
  • Eye discomfort
  • Headaches
  • Joint pain or muscle pain
  • Multivitamins
  • Runny Nose
  • Sore Throats
  • Vomiting
  • Wheezing
  • Skin rashes

For donating your medicine go to contact page and fill the form.
